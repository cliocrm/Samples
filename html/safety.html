  <h3 style="font-size:3.5vw;color:white;">Technology for Personal Safety and Crime Control</h3>


<h3>

Safety has different definitions at different places - at home, on the road, at the workplace, in public gatherings, inside a cab, etc. depending on who might pose a threat. There are several products like pepper sprays for defending ourselves. Hardly any of them are effective when it comes to some really dangerous crimes where the individual is attacked by a group of armed criminals. <br/>

Children and senior citizens are also vulnerable but not as much as women and girls. Organizations fighting for their rights are many, but only when they come as victims. Till then it is difficult to convince anyone, including those who are responsible for their safety, that they need security. Considering this situation, it is important that, we protect ourselves with the most effective and reliable means.<br/>

Smartphones are the electronic devices most popularly used by individuals for surfing, playing games, and other utilities. Safety comes much later in our priority list because it's a one-off situation. But considering the current situation and tactics of criminals, it is wise to get help as soon as a person feels threatened by an individual or a group. <br/>

The most obvious means is to send an alert to family, friends, police, and others as effectively as possible. Stalking online and offline isn't uncommon and women find it hard to get a solution to this issue due to weak cyber police resolutions and help available. The ultimate goal of these criminals is usually to attack the victim physically after hampering the personal, professional, and financial lives of the victims, acid attacks for example, or rape and molestation. It is always wise to keep the family on alert whenever outside or any other place not just by calling but a faster means that can reach multiple people and groups closest to the person as quickly as possible.<br/>

Here are some examples of applications:<br/>

1. The lock screen is designed to show information that interests the users but can be converted to a useful means of faster communication. Sending alerts via automated calls, SMS, WhatsApp message, or any other means.<br/><br/>

Typically in my view, the application should have a Panic Button that connects with an Alerting System that uses the mobile network for sending the necessary details like latitude/longitude, etc. and customized messages to a list of Emergency Contacts (Family members/Nearest police station/NGOs, etc.). Also, a robust system might want to track the person in the absence of mobile internet services or low battery power like a black box. Storing the trail of the person and tracking it in real-time is also an important addition.<br/>

2. A personal CCTV with a spare smartphone to track activities at home/office space etc. in your absence is a useful application of WebRTC for mobile phones. A central server for storing the streaming video for playback at a later time should be enabled for the application.<br/>

This is useful for parents, pet owners, or anyone who wants to ensure that no unwanted person enters their personal space or does anything suspicious with their loved ones or belongings.<br/>

The phone's front or back camera or both can be used if positioned properly.<br/>

3. A volunteering circle of men and women who are experts in martial arts, having licensed weapons for protection, in collaboration with local government and administrative bodies can help women, children, differently-abled, and aged people in distress. The same can be connected with a lock screen application like the one explained above.

Crimes alter or end the lives of the victims and affect the surrounding people in many ways. No bully has the right to dictate to us what we should be doing in our lives. Ending crimes is a difficult task since it is directly linked to the human mind and psychology that is so complex that we haven't yet understood it fully. Most of the time, there exists a motive behind such actions, but at other times, human emotions lead to destructive behavior. It is often difficult to explain how difficulties with self might lead to problems for others. It is always desirable to have an education system that trains people to be peace-loving and to love themselves, but due to the various socio-economic factors, upbringing, etc., there are people who either have the tendency to do wrong or don't see any issue in harming others. <br/>

Corruption is a very common cause of crimes against people of various social statuses. Other social evils like prostitution, human trafficking, bullying, honor killings, etc. have plagued our society for centuries. The only way is to control it in the best possible way and for that reporting at the right time is very crucial. Electronic devices need to be free from malware both at the application level and in the system software installed. The microphone or camera and other input components like the keyboard should not be used for stalking the user. <br/>

Blockchain, AI and ML, security algorithms, biometrics, quantum cryptography, and other advanced and intelligent systems can help prevent any such crimes from happening. Most importantly the human instinct and understanding the psychology of criminals is important for preventing crimes. <br/>

AI can be used to figure out the trends of serial murders and other organized crimes that target a particular section of people in a very systematic planned manner. <br/>

We need more and more sophisticated techniques because the criminals are also educating themselves to commit crimes in the most fool-proof way. It is not just the professional commitment that is important to solve such cases but also a personal interest in saving lives and property from being destroyed. Hence, I feel any person who is involved only in preventing crimes or catching criminals need to have a personal desire towards righteousness, from experience. Then techniques, methods, and innovation will be seen more often in this field.
