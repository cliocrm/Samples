4. Due to the naiveness of people they often get misled by social engineers who might portray themselves as someone else. For instance, a middle aged IT professional, might act like a prestigious and respectful person or even a teen aged girl to leak information!
5. Social engineers are vulnerable to bad habits and OCD symptoms, but are shrewd at the same time. They understand the ways to "hire" other social engineers and cyber criminals from within the friends or family circle of the victim.
6. Only way to fight such people is to openly talk about all interactions with them. They might have political backing, so it is important to be careful while dealing with such criminals.
